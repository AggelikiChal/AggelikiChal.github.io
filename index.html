About me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science of Reykjavik University. I got my PhD from the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering of National Technical University of Athens under the supervision of Aris Pagourtzis. My research interests include computational complexity and logic in computer science. In specific, I am interested in the structural and descriptive complexity of counting problems, and the complexity of modal logics with applications in formal verification.
