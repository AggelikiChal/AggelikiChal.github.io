I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science of Reykjavik University. I got my PhD from the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering of NTUA under the supervision of Aris Pagourtzis and Stathis Zachos. My research interests include computational complexity and logic in computer science, and in specific, complexity of counting problems, descriptive complexity, and complexity of modal logics.
